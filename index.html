What is Performance Marketing?​How often should I wash my car?

A form of digital advertising where advertisers only pay when a specific result occurs, such as a click, lead, sale, or app install.

What platforms are used in Performance Marketing?

Key platforms include Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Linked in, Affiliate Networks, and various Display Ad Exchanges.

How does Performance Marketing ensure a return on investment (ROI)?

ROI is guaranteed because the budget is entirely focused on results-driven strategies. We track every action to ensure maximum efficiency from every Rupee spent.

How quickly will I see results?

We deliver actionable performance data within 4 weeks. Expect to hit your efficient, scalable growth targets in 2 to 3 months as optimization compounds.

Do I need a large budget to start?

Not necessarily. Performance marketing is scalable. You can start with a test budget to prove your Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) before increasing spend.
